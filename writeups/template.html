Reconnaissance

Initial scanning was performed using Nmap to identify open ports and services running on the target system.

└─$ sudo nmap -p- -T4 TARGET_IP
Thought process: why -p- and -T4?

Running with -p- scans all 65535 ports instead of just the top 1000. This catches non-standard services. -T4 speeds things up on a fast lab connection. On a real engagement, a slower timing would reduce noise.
